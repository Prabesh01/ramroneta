About Our Voter Information Platform

Empowering voters with transparent candidate information for informed democratic decisions

Our Mission

We are a group of independent researchers from different districts across the nation, aiming to provide voters with information about their candidates so that they can make informed decisions.

Use this platform to:

  • Know who is competing in this election from your locality and whether they deserve your vote or not.
  • Know what kind of candidates the parties have chosen as their representatives and whether you should cast vote on their flag or not.

We believe, if voters are informed about candidate's background and history, the act of voting blindly based on who's more popular or voting the party that parents or even the whole village supports will be heavily minimized. Whom we vote determines how our country and our future will be shaped. Let's elect the good and capable candidate. Do not waste your vote on assholes who are not worthy of it.
