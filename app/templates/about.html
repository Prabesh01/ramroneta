About Us

We are a group of independent researchers from different districts across the nation, aiming to provide voters with information about their candidates so that they can make informed decisions.

Our Mission

We believe, if voters are informed about a candidate's background and history, the act of blindly voting based on popularity or party loyalty will be minimized.

Use this platform to:

✔ Know who is competing in this election from your locality and whether they deserve your vote or not.
✔ Know what kind of candidates parties have chosen as their representatives and whether you should cast vote on their flag or not.

Whom we vote determines how our country and our future will be shaped. Let's elect good and capable candidates. Do not waste your vote on people who are not worthy of it.
